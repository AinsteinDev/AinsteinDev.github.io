Wild West RPG

A choice-driven open-world RPG set across multiple acts. Shape your destiny in the lawless frontier.

About the Game

In Wild West RPG, you’ll navigate through dusty towns, lawless deserts, and fractured territories across a world divided by acts. Each act brings a new region, characters, and consequences. Explore freely, complete quests, and make decisions that affect the future of the frontier.

Features

  • 🔥 Open world structure with act-based regions
  • 🤠 Choices matter — every action shapes your story
  • 🔫 Skill-based combat and RPG progression
  • 🏕️ Side quests, secrets, and factions to align with or betray
  • 📱 Optimized for mobile, future plans for Switch
