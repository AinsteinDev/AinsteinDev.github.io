About the Game

Wild West RPG is a top-down open world game where your choices matter. Set across multiple acts, each with its own sandbox map, you'll shape your path as a bounty hunter, outlaw, or hero in the untamed frontier.

Features

Act-Based Open World

Each act is a unique region with its own quests, factions, and secrets.

Choice-Driven Gameplay

Your decisions shape the story and world events permanently.

Top-Down RPG Style

Classic RPG mechanics with exploration, inventory, and upgrades.
